# Autonex Work From Home (WFH) Policy

## 1. Weekly Quota
Full-time employees are entitled to **1 Work From Home (WFH) day per week**. 

## 2. Clubbing and Carryover
- Unused WFH days can be carried over and clubbed together.
- The maximum number of banked WFH days an employee can hold at any given time is **4 days**.
- WFH balances reset at the end of every calendar month. You cannot carry over banked WFH days from one month to the next.

## 3. Application Process
- WFH requests must be submitted via the portal.
- Approval from the Project Manager is required.
- WFH is a privilege, and approval is contingent on project deliverables and performance.

## 4. Interns
Interns are generally expected to work from the office to maximize learning opportunities. WFH for interns is granted only under special circumstances with PM approval.
