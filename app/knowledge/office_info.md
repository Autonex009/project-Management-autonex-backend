# Autonex Office Information

## 1. Location & Access
- **Address**: Level 5, Tech Park, Main Tech Hub.
- **Access Cards**: Mandatory for entry. If lost, report immediately to IT/Admin. 
- **Visitor Policy**: All visitors must be pre-registered at the front desk.

## 2. Working Hours
- Standard working hours are **9:00 AM to 6:00 PM**, Monday to Friday.
- A mandatory 1-hour lunch break is included.
- Core collaboration hours are **10:30 AM to 4:00 PM**. Please ensure you are available for meetings during this window.

## 3. Facilities
- **Cafeteria**: Located on the 4th floor. Open from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
- **Meeting Rooms**: Must be booked in advance via the calendar system. Please clean up after use.
- **Parking**: Employees must register their vehicle number with Admin to receive a parking sticker.
