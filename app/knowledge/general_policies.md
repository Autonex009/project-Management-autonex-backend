# Autonex General Policies & Do's and Don'ts

## 1. Code of Conduct
- **Respect**: Treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with respect. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.
- **Integrity**: Always act with honesty and transparency. Report any conflicts of interest immediately to HR.

## 2. Do's
- **Do** keep your manager informed of any roadblocks early.
- **Do** secure your workstation when stepping away (Win+L or Cmd+Control+Q).
- **Do** use company-approved tools and software for all project work.
- **Do** dress professionally when meeting clients, whether in-person or virtually.

## 3. Don'ts
- **Don't** share confidential client data, passwords, or intellectual property on public channels or with unauthorized individuals.
- **Don't** use unauthorized third-party software for company work without IT approval.
- **Don't** engage in side-gigs or secondary employment that conflicts with your duties at Autonex.
- **Don't** use offensive or inappropriate language in company communications.

## 4. IT & Security
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
- Report any suspicious emails or security breaches to the IT Helpdesk immediately.
- Never leave printed documents containing sensitive information on your desk overnight.
