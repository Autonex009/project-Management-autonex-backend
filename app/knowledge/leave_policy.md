# Autonex Leave Policy

## 1. Annual Quotas
- **Paid Leave (PL)**: 12 days per year. Accrued annually.
- **Casual/Sick Leave (CL/SL)**: 6 days per year combined. Cannot be carried forward.
- **Floater Leave**: 2 days per year. Must be taken on pre-approved floater holiday dates.
- **Interns**: Interns do not get the standard annual quota. They accrue 1 Paid Leave per month. This resets monthly.

## 2. Leave Application
All leave requests must be submitted through the Autonex PM Portal. Leaves must be approved by the reporting Project Manager (PM). If a PM applies for leave, it is routed to Admin for approval.

## 3. Clubbing Rules
- Employees may combine different types of leaves.
- Leaves exceeding 5 consecutive days require special management approval.
- Combining leaves with public holidays and weekends is permitted and encouraged for better work-life balance.

## 4. Quota Exhaustion
Once an employee exhausts their paid leave quota, any additional time off will be treated as Leave Without Pay (LWP). A per-day salary deduction will apply for LWP.

## 5. Notice Period
- For leaves of 1-2 days, a minimum of 24 hours notice is required.
- For leaves exceeding 3 days, a minimum of 7 days notice is required.
